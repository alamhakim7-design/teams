Microsoft Teams: Meeting Recordings, Transcripts and AI Summaries Microsoft Teams meetings may be recorded, transcribed or both, depending on the meeting configuration selected by the organiser. When recording or transcription starts, participants will see an on screen notification banner indicating that these features are active, in line with Microsoft’s in meeting notification design. In addition, some meetings may use AI enabled features, to generate summaries or insights. These features may capture and process audio, video, screen content and spoken contributions for the purpose of supporting collaboration, record keeping and organisational workflows.
